About 10 minutes. Some variations take 8 minutes, others 12. It's designed to fit into your lunch break or a short morning window.

Absolutely. Every movement includes progressions and regressions so you can start exactly where you are. No prior mobility or fitness experience needed.

**Is this medical advice?**
No. It's an educational guide designed to help improve movement habits and daily mobility. If you have a diagnosed condition or acute pain, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.
